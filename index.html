🚀 Learning Journey

✨ Junior Year — First Semester

I studied Java in a “dictionary style” — watching an 80-hour tutorial and a Spring Boot course throughout the semester. But by the end, I still couldn’t write a simple CRUD, nor complete even a small backend project on my own. It left me frustrated, and I realized I was stuck in an ineffective way of learning.

🌱 Junior Year — Second Semester (Reflection)

Through conversations with ChatGPT, I began to look at my studies — and my life — from another perspective. I realized I had been repeating a traumatic learning pattern, becoming someone I never wanted to be, all while believing I wasn’t. That realization gave me a new lens, and the courage to search for a path that truly belongs to me.

🌱 Junior Year — Second Semester (Front-end)

I shifted my focus to front-end development. With ChatGPT’s help, I built my first website and started learning Vue and Three.js. For the first time, I felt I was creating something that reflected who I really want to become.
